About GENSETS

Coimbatore's Most Trusted Generator Parts Supplier

Established in 2009, GENSETS has grown to become the most trusted name in generator spare parts supply across Coimbatore and Tamil Nadu. We supply premium quality OEM and OEM-equivalent parts to industrial facilities, hospitals, hotels, data centers, and manufacturing plants throughout India.

Our deep technical expertise in generator systems — from Cummins and Kirloskar to Deep Sea, InteliGen, Leroy-Somer, and Stamford — means you always get the right part, verified for compatibility before dispatch. We don't just sell parts; we solve power reliability problems.

From a single critical spare to bulk procurement contracts, GENSETS delivers with speed, precision, and the kind of technical support that prevents costly downtime.
