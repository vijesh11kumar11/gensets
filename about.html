About GENSETS

25 years of industrial power expertise from the heart of Coimbatore

Who We Are

Built on Engineering.
Driven by Reliability.

Established in 2000, GENSETS has grown from a specialist generator spare parts dealer in Coimbatore into one of South India's most trusted industrial power component suppliers. We were founded by engineers for engineers — people who understand that downtime is never an option when critical power infrastructure is at stake.

From our base in Ganapathy, Coimbatore, we supply genuine and OEM-equivalent spare parts across the length and breadth of India. Our deep relationships with leading generator manufacturers, combined with an inventory spanning 500+ products, means we can source what you need — fast. Hospitals, hotels, data centers, factories, and telecom installations rely on us to keep their generators running.

What sets GENSETS apart is our technical backbone. Every member of our team carries hands-on experience with diesel generator systems. We don't just dispatch parts — we verify compatibility, advise on fitment, and stand behind every component we supply. After 15 years, our reputation rests on a single promise: the right part, at the right time, every time.

Our Story

Our Journey

From a single office in Coimbatore to serving 1000+ clients across India — here's how we got here.

2000
Foundation

GENSETS was established in Ganapathy, Coimbatore by a team of generator engineers with a vision to become the most reliable spare parts supplier in South India. Operations began with a focused inventory of critical DG set components.

2012
Product Expansion

We expanded our product catalogue to include Automatic Voltage Regulators (AVRs), digital controllers, electronic governors, and fuel actuators — becoming a one-stop source for generator control and regulation components.

2015
Industrial Clientele

GENSETS began supplying to large-scale industrial customers including hospitals, five-star hotels, data centers, and manufacturing facilities — sectors where reliable power is mission-critical and downtime carries severe consequences.

2018
Pan-India Growth

We extended our supply chain to serve clients across all major Indian states. Improved logistics partnerships and an upgraded warehousing system enabled faster dispatch with reliable delivery timelines to customers nationwide.

2021
Services Launch

Recognising our customers' need for deeper technical support, GENSETS launched a dedicated maintenance consultation service. Our engineers now advise on preventive maintenance schedules, part replacement intervals, and system optimisation.

2024
Today

GENSETS now serves 1000+ clients with a product range exceeding 500 SKUs across 50+ industries. We continue to invest in inventory depth, technical training, and logistics infrastructure — always with the same founding commitment to reliability.
